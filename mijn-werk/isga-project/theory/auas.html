AUAS Context

In your time at AUAS, you might not only find terms in Dutch that you don’t know. You may also not understand the translation of them. This course will focus on defining various terms you may come across in your time at AUAS.

Other terms that don’t need a definition can be found in the word list.

Fundament

Foundation

Most studies talk about a foundation stage. This stage is all about building the foundation on your study and understanding the basics.

Profilering

Profiling

Profiling is the stage in a study where you start specialising in a certain field within your study. This can contain stages (internships), studying abroad, or simply picking a set subject in the field you wish to specialise in.

Afstuderen

Graduation

This is the final stage of a study and is all about graduating from your study. You’ll often find that some final project or research paper is involved in this year.

Studieregiepunt/
professiona-
liseringspunt

Professionalism point

These points are about doing courses or work outside of your study to try and develop yourself. This work towards self-development does have to tie within your study.

Studiepunt (EC)

Study point

Your work is graded from a scale of 1-10, this result is then converted into study points. These points are what you need to collect to pass the year.

Propedeuse

Propaedeutics

This is what the first-year of an AUAS study is called.

Studieloopbaan-
begeleider (SLB-er)

Study career guide

A SLB-er gives the subject SLB. A SLB-er is there to discuss your progress, ambitions, or any problems and how you plan to solve or succeed in those things. You can also contact your SLB-er if you have a problem with a teacher that can’t be solved with the teacher themselves.

Decaan

Dean

A studentendecaan is someone you can go to for problems on a more personal level. They are a trust person for you talk about any personal problems that may affect your study. Stuff such as mental health/illness, problems at home, illness, and any doubts you may have about your choice of study.
