Some handy links

Do you want to learn more Dutch? Check out these courses!

ISN Amsterdam

ISN Amsterdam offers 12 week courses where you will learn how to indoruce yourself, how to ask for directions, and lots more. The 12 week course costs €160, this includes a workbook worth €24.95.

For more information, check their website: www.isnamsterdam.nl/activities/dutch-lessons-2.

Duolingo

You can also start a Dutch language course on Doulingo. Go to www.duolingo.com and click on 'Dutch'. It is a free resource with lessons varrying from beginners tips to fun vocabulary.
