Avatar: The Game

Process

  • During the whole schoolyear I had tried to choose topics that I myself found interesting, that was also the case for this project. I’m a big fan of Avatar: The Last Airbender and I’ve always wanted to play the series as a game. For this assignment I took my chance.

  • Actually, from the beginning, I was pretty ambitious with my idea, because of this the coding side of things proved to be difficult. What I really struggled with was saving data through JavaScript across various pages in the website. In the game you have to train to then apply that information in another page. Additionally, it was a bit of a chore to make the user be able to do a “move” by pressing a specific button on their keyboard.

Assignment

Design and make a website through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The website has to contain functionalities inspired by Tamagotchi.

Reflection

Looking back, I would like to work out my concept some more. Mainly a fighting system would make the game feel complete. Essentially, more functionalities such as choosing your own bending element before playing the game.

Other than that, it’s mostly cleaning up the code and applying the knowledge I have now to make it work more efficiently.
